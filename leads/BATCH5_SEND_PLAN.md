## How to work this batch

1. Open a draft, read it, tweak the opening line if you know something
   specific about that business (it lifts reply rates a lot).
2. For the "ready to email" group: paste the body into a new Gmail (so your
   real signature auto-inserts) and send.
3. For the "contact form / WhatsApp" group: paste the body into their form or
   WhatsApp — the plain-text signature is already in the draft.
4. When you send one, update its row in `tracker.csv`: status → `sent`,
   fill `date_contacted`. When someone replies, status → `replied` and note
   the follow-up.
5. Pair this with the WhatsApp openers in
   `growmatic-sa-backend/docs/GrowMatic_Sales_Playbook.md` for the ones you
   reach on WhatsApp — shorter is better there.

**10 a day. Every day.** That's the whole game.
